Q: HOW DO I BUY NEX? +

Install Freighter (Chrome/Firefox) or Lobstr (mobile). Add a trustline to NEX. Then buy on the Stellar DEX (NEX/USDC). Trades settle in ~5 seconds.

Q: WHAT IS NEX BACKED BY? +

Every wrapped NEX on Stellar is backed 1:1 by native NEX held in a publicly auditable post-quantum custody wallet. The bridge always holds at least as much native NEX as wrapped NEX in circulation. Verify the reserve anytime.

Q: CAN I MINE NEX? +

Yes. NEX uses SHA-256d proof-of-work — any Bitcoin ASIC works (Bitaxe, Antminer, Whatsminer). Solo mine with 0% pool fee at stratum+tcp://98.80.98.17:3333. Block reward is 100 NEX per block, paid directly to your address.

Q: WHAT MAKES NEX POST-QUANTUM? +

NEX enforces ML-DSA-65 (FIPS 204 Dilithium3) signatures at the consensus layer. Every spendable output on the KnexCoin chain requires a real post-quantum signature — classical ECDSA outputs are rejected by every node. This protects against future quantum computer attacks on the chain's cryptography.

Q: IS THE NETWORK REALLY RUNNING? +

Yes. Live mainnet since genesis. Miners are producing blocks every ~5 minutes. Watch the live block stream on the untraceablex.com explorer. The network is real, the blocks are real, the miners are real.

Q: CAN I REDEEM WRAPPED NEX FOR NATIVE NEX? +

Yes. Go to bridge.knexcoin.com, connect Freighter, enter your native NEX address, and burn your wrapped NEX. The bridge pays out native NEX from the reserve within minutes. For questions, message @oterodavid on Telegram.
