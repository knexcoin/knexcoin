Cookie Controls.

What we store in your browser, why, and how to turn it off. Last updated May 2026.

Effective: 1 May 2026 · Operator: Distributed Ledger Technologies

The short version

We use as few cookies as we can. We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking pixels. Most cookies you'll see here are first-party and exist to make the site work or to count traffic in aggregate.

What we do not use

  • Advertising cookies of any kind.
  • Cross-site tracking pixels (Meta, X, Google Ads, etc.).
  • Session replay or behavioral recording.
  • Fingerprinting libraries.

Turn it off

You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings at any time. Required cookies are needed for the site to function — if you block them, some features will break.

To opt out of analytics specifically, use your browser's Do Not Track or Global Privacy Control setting. We honor both.

To clear what's already stored:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Clear browsing data.
  • Safari: Settings → Privacy → Manage Website Data.
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data.

Changes

If we add a new cookie or change what an existing one does, we'll update this page and the date at the top.
